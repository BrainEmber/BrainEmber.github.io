Mancala is a turn based board game where each player is trying to move stones or seeds from the board into their mancala. (the long goal on each end of the board)
During a turn the player picks a hole and takes out all of the stones, then places one stone in each subsiquent hole until there are no more stones left to place.
If during your turn your last stone is dropped into your mancala then you get another turn
When there are no more stones on the board the game is over and whoever has the most in their mancala is the winner!
